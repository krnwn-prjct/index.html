CHAPTER I

The Wind

No one chooses the wind that carries their life.

Some are carried toward dreams. Some toward loss. Some toward places they never imagined.

Yet every dandelion seed continues to fly. Not because it knows where it will land. But because it trusts the wind.

CHAPTER II

The Journey

Life was never about avoiding storms.

It was about learning that storms also move us.

Every difficult day. Every goodbye. Every failure. Every beginning. They all become part of the same journey.

CHAPTER III

The Promise

I cannot promise that life will always be kind.

But I can promise to continue growing.

Even when the wind changes. Even when the path disappears. Even when I stand alone.

I will continue.
