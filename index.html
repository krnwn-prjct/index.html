Promise of the Dandelions

Some promises are never spoken loudly. They are carried quietly, like dandelion seeds, waiting for the right wind.

This page is not a portfolio. It is not a diary. It is not a place to seek admiration. This is simply a promise. A reminder to myself that no matter how difficult the journey becomes, I will continue walking. Even if everyone leaves. Even if everything changes. Even if I lose almost everything. I will not lose the person I promised to become.

— Ryan
